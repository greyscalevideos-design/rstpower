Why Powerduct Pro

Built for uptime, verified by test

Every Powerduct Pro system is factory-assembled and independently certified — not just designed to a standard, but proven against it under third-party test conditions.

🏅

DEKRA third-party certification

Independent verification of thermal, mechanical and electrical performance — not self-declared compliance. DEKRA is one of the world's leading testing and certification organisations.

🔥

Fire integrity to 830°C

120-minute fire resistance under IEC 60331-1:2009, with BS 6387:2013 C/W/Z compliance for circuit integrity in fire conditions — essential for life safety and critical power circuits.

🔌

Live tap-off flexibility

Plug-in tap-off units allow circuits to be added or relocated without de-energising the main run — critical for live data centre environments where downtime is not an option.

💧

Gel resin insulation

Superior dielectric performance and moisture resistance compared to air-insulated alternatives — suited to humid, demanding plant environments and cleanroom facilities.

🛡️

Steel-clad outer enclosure

Robust mechanical protection and effective EMC shielding — lower electromagnetic field emission than equivalent cable systems, important in sensitive electronic environments.

📊

Scalable current range

Available from 400A to 5000A to suit distribution boards, rising mains and high-capacity trunk feeders across all facility types — with aluminium and copper conductor options.

Thermal Performance

Thermal efficiency in the data centre

IEC 61439-6 mandates temperature-rise verification at rated current. Powerduct Pro's flat copper conductor geometry produces lower I²R losses per metre than equivalent cable, reducing parasitic heat load on cooling infrastructure and contributing directly to improved PUE.

Applications

Where Powerduct Pro is specified

Designed for environments where power distribution reliability, fire performance and thermal management are non-negotiable.

01

Data centres & hyperscale

Rising mains, distribution to UPS and PDU, high-density row power — with live tap-off capability for future capacity expansion without taking the system offline.

02

Life sciences & pharma

Cleanroom-compatible, low-EMF, IP55-rated construction for GMP facilities where contamination control and system traceability are essential requirements.

03

BESS & energy storage

High-current DC and AC distribution for battery energy storage systems, with fire-rated options where containment is required by regulations or client specification.

04

Industrial & manufacturing

Robust steel enclosure and certified short-circuit ratings for process plant, heavy industrial and high-availability production environments across Ireland and internationally.
